Nouns:
	NOUNd noun in regular, dictionary form
	NOUNa noun prefixed with a/an
	NOUNds noun in plural form
	
Verbs:
	VERBd verb in regular, dictionary form (I hack the computer)
	VERBed verb in past tense (I hacked the computer)
	VERBs verb in present tense (He hacks the computer)
	VERBobj verb for when the noun is the object of the sentence (The computer got hacked)
	VERBing verb in present progressive (I am hacking the computer)
	VERBn verb in noun form (I got the computer with a hack)
